A Cosmic 'Off Switch': Could the Universe Stop Expanding?

Click here for more details

According to published articles by Paul Sutter on the Live Science website, particularly one entitled "Could the universe ever stop expanding? New theory proposes a cosmic 'off switch'", scientists are now exploring the possibility that the universe’s expansion might not be eternal. The mysterious force known as Dark Energy—which drives the accelerated expansion of the cosmos—could potentially undergo what researchers call “violent transitions.” These sudden changes in state may act as a kind of cosmic "off switch," reversing the universe’s growth and possibly leading to a contraction phase. This new theory opens up profound questions about the ultimate fate of the universe and challenges long-held assumptions about its future trajectory.
